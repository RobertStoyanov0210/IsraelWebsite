ABOUT ISRAEL
  • Area: 20,770 sq km (about the size of New Jersey)
  • Population: 8,299,706 (July 2017 est, includes Golan Heights and East Jerusalem)
  • Median age: 29.9
  • Capital: Israel declared Jerusalem as its capital in 1950
  • Ethnic Groups: Jewish 74.7%, non-Jewish 25.2% (mostly Arab)
MORE INTERESTING FACTS
  • Israel has the eighth longest life expectancy in the world: 82 years
  • With more than 3,000 high-tech companies and start-ups, Israel has the highest concentration of hi-tech companies in the world (apart from the Silicon Valley).
  • About 93 percent of Israeli homes use solar energy for water heating, the highest percentage in the world.
  • There are over 100 sushi restaurants in Tel Aviv, which makes it the city with the most sushi restaurants per capita after Tokyo and NYC.
  • Israeli banknotes have Braille markings on them.
